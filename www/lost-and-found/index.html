Lost and Found

Please remember to take all your belongings when you leave the Community Center. We lock up the building when we leave about 30-45 minutes after the end of the dance. Any items that we find in the ballroom we leave on the coat rack at the top of the stairs. The doors are generally open at Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM to 3:30 PM. The Community Center will eventually dispose of things that have been hanging around for over a month or so.
