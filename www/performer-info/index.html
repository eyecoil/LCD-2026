Performer information

Stage

Equipment available for performers

We provide 2 monitors for the band with seperate mixes. Dynamic and condenser mics and short/regular mic stande. You can use upto 16 or more XLR inputs. Phantom power available individually to any input. We have 2 DI boxes. Our PA is always a stereo mix. We also use subwoofers as needed (aux fed)

What should you bring.

Mute pedals, 1/4 inch cables.

Room size and capacity

Capacity is around 100

informtion for callers

Callers can choose between a wired mic and a wireless mic (both are Shure SM58). We provide a caller monitor. The caller position is stage right.
