Lenox Community Center
65 Walker Street, Lenox Massachusetts

From the North (Pittsfield, MA): Take Rt. 7 south. Turn right on to 7A into Lenox. At the intersection with 183 at the south end of the village swing to the left to continue on 7A. You'll come to where 7A bears to the right, and 183 North is straight ahead. The LCC is directly on the left.

From the South/East (Lee, MA, or points East from I-90):Exit 2 off I-90 at Lee. Turn right onto Rt. 20. Continue through the village of Lee. At intersection with Rt. 7 continue to the next traffic light at the intersection of 183 (Walker St.) Turn left onto Walker St., and travel .8 miles. At the intersection of 183 and 7A South coming in from the left, the Lenox Community Center will be immediately at your right.

From the South (Stockbridge, MA):Take Rt. 7 north. Turn left on 7A into Lenox. At the intersection of 7A and 183. The LCC will be across the street to your right.

From the South/West (W. Stockbridge, MA, or New York State):Exit B3 off I-90. Turn right on to Rt. 22-South. Little more than 1/2 mile, turn left on NY 958 which becomes MA102 for West Stockbridge. About 2 1/2 miles from West Stockbridge. Look for a left turn onto 183 (Berkshire Botanical Garden on your left, signs for Rockwell Museum on the right). Take 183 into Lenox. Continue straight through the intersection to join with 7A South. You'll come to where 7A bears to the right, and 183 North is straight ahead. The LCC is directly on the left.
